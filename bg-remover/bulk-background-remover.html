Best Practices for Organizing Bulk Processed Images

After downloading your processed images, follow these organizational tips to streamline your e-commerce workflow:

File Naming Conventions

Use consistent naming patterns like:

  • SKU-123_product-name_removed.png
  • category-item-001_removed.png

This makes it easy to match processed images with your inventory management system.

Folder Structure

Organize by collection or category:

  • /products/electronics/
  • /products/apparel/
  • /products/home-goods/

Quality Control Checklist

Before uploading to your store, verify:

  1. All product edges are clean (no halos or artifacts)
  2. Background is pure white (#FFFFFF) or fully transparent
  3. Image resolution meets marketplace requirements (Amazon: 1000px minimum on longest side)
  4. File sizes are optimized (compress if needed without losing quality)

Backup Your Originals

Always keep a copy of your original product photos before processing. Store them in a separate "originals" folder in case you need to reprocess with different settings later.
