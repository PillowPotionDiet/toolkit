How to Use

1

Download & Install

Download the app above and run the installer. On first run, Windows may show a security warning - click "More info" then "Run anyway".

2

Get Your FTP Credentials

Find your FTP details in Hostinger hPanel:

  1. Go to hpanel.hostinger.com
  2. Click on any website
  3. Go to Files ‚Üí FTP Accounts
  4. Copy the Host, Username, and Password
3

Connect & Select Sites

Enter your FTP credentials in the app and click Connect. The app will scan and show all your site folders. Select the ones you want to download.

4

Download!

Click "Start Download" and watch the progress. Files are saved to your Desktop in a migration-backups folder, organized by domain.
